In an old time a farmer killed another person with a sickle, so people went toward the elder man of the village for justice. The elder man order to collect all of the sickles and be kept in front of him.then he picked up a sickle and said its owner is a murderer. The people were very surprised and they asked “how did you knew the murderer”? Then he reply that the bee had only landed on his sickle than the other. Even it was washed of but the effects of the blood still remain.

Similarly, fingerprints which the criminal leaves somewhere, are enough to identify. Similarly, if we found a dead body in water, that also known that he died by drowning in the water or someone hit him and threw him into the water. If water comes out of his stomach then he is dead in water, and if no water comes out then he has been killed by someone else. Similarly, the criminal can be identified through blood group.

But in all these methods there is a doubt that the real criminal is someone else. So machines have been invented to remove the doubt and clearly identify the criminal because the real criminal can be identify by machine, and there is no room for doubt.
